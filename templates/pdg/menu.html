Garlic Butter Shrimp Fettuccine
$12
Ingredients
  • 250g fettuccine
  • 300g shrimp (peeled & deveined)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 30g butter
  • 100ml heavy cream
  • 50g Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, fresh parsley (chopped)
Recipe
  1. Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions.
  2. Melt butter with olive oil, sauté garlic.
  3. Add shrimp, season, cook until pink.
  4. Pour in cream & Parmesan, stir.
  5. Add pasta, toss, garnish with parsley.
  6. Serve hot with extra Parmesan.
